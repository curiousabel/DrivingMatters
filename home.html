About Me

I have over 23 years of experience. My Career began as transport driver with approximately 500,000 miles travelling long haul through mountain passes and steep grades throughout United States and Canada. I Became a certified in car/transport instructor. I worked as a safety and Compliance Supervisor at Verspeeten Cartage overseeing 400 drivers. I furthered my career as a Certified Driver Examiner at the Ministry of Transport/ Drive Test for the past 16 years with the ability to test G1 ,G2, A , B, C ,D , E, E ,F and Z Class of licences

I became very interested in forming my own business when I realized that from the age of 16 to 79 years of age that there was no additional training available unless you upgraded to a different class pf licence . 63 years of driving errors un-noticed by everyday road users. It became even more clear when ii started asking people when was the last time they picked up a current driver’s handbook. They generally replied” not since I got my licence” I continued researching insurance rates and premium and was shocked to see that Ontario was the second highest across the Provinces. I felt it was time to begin with one Driver at time in hopes of one day reducing these premium through quality driver training and getting the driver’s hand book back into the hand of the driver.
